Test Case 1: Finding the cheapest bicycle on amazon.
Testing search functionality on amazon.com.

1. Open a new browser in incognito mode
3. Open "amazon.com"
4. Find searching bar
5. Click it, so you can type in
6. Type "bicycle"
7. Hit "Enter" or click on loop button
8. Find "Sort by" button
9. Click it
10. From shown menu choose "Price: Low to High"
11. Expect list of bicycles sorted ascending by price.
